Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2025

At Tweakr, your privacy is a top priority. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Tweakr does not collect or store any personal information. Messages you copy or type are sent to our AI service in real time to generate replies and translations — they are never stored on our servers.

How Your Data Is Used

Text you submit is used solely to generate AI replies and translations in real time. Once a response is generated, your text is immediately discarded. We do not build profiles, store message history, or analyze your content for any other purpose.

Third Party Services

Anthropic (Claude AI) — Tweakr uses Anthropic's Claude AI to generate replies and translations. Your messages are sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Please review Anthropic's privacy policy.

RevenueCat — Tweakr uses RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymized purchase data. Please review RevenueCat's privacy policy.

Data Security

All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. We use industry-standard security practices to protect any data in transit.

Keyboard Extension & Full Access

Tweakr requires "Allow Full Access" to function as a keyboard extension. This permission is required by iOS for keyboard extensions to access the internet (needed to call the AI API). We do not log, store, or transmit anything you type — only the specific text you copy and submit for AI reply generation.

Children's Privacy

Tweakr is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at support@tweakr.app
